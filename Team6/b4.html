South Indian Platter

In India, every family has its own recipes for day-to-day and festival foods - sometimes the recipes are passed down for generations. It is possible to zero in on a single perfect recipe or the most authentic recipe..... takr the pleases your plate !!!

Idlies are very popular throught India.Sooji Idlies are steam cooked savory cakes of fermented batter of Semolina(sooji/rava). Usually in South India Idlies are served with sambhar and chutneys fot the breakfast, however one can make it for any meal. Idlies are very delicious and also healthy as they are steam cooked.

Dosasare fine pancakes of fermented lentil and rice batter. Dosas can be made with different flavours by adding the stuffings, such as - masala dosa, Onion dosa, Paneer dosa, etc.....Dosa is served with sambhar and a variety of chutneys.

Tomato Rice os very popular rice preperation from Southern India. Homegrown heirloom variety of tomatoes has been used in here. Tomato rice can be served with either sambhar or chutney.

Vada is a category of savoury fried snacks from India. Different types of vadas can be described variously as fritters, cutlets, doughnuts, or dumplings. Alternative names for this food include wada, vade, vadai, wadeh and bara.
